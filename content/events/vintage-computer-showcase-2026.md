Join us for the Treasure Valley Vintage Computing Club's first Vintage Computer Showcase! Come see vintage hardware in action, meet fellow enthusiasts, and share your love of retro computing.

**When:** Saturday, April 18th, 2026 from 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM
